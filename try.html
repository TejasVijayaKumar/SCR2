Bangalore Tourist Places

Bangalore, the "Garden City," is a vibrant mix of history, culture, and modernity. Explore its iconic food spots and serene visiting places.

Food Spots

Vidyarthi Bhavan: Iconic vegetarian eatery famous for its dosa.

MTR, Lalbagh: Must-visit for authentic South Indian cuisine.

VV Puram Food Street: A paradise for food lovers.

Visiting Places

Dodda Ganapathi Temple: A historic temple with spiritual significance.

Lalbagh: A sprawling botanical garden with beautiful landscapes.

MG Road: A bustling shopping and entertainment hub.

Cubbon Park: A green oasis in the heart of the city.
